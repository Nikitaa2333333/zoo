## Behavioral Rules
1. **Tone:** Premium, caring, professional.
2. **Design Constraints:** 
   - NO CAPS in any text elements.
   - NO high-performance/heavy animations.
   - Design consistency with the "Design Code" (to be developed).
3. **Approval:** ANY change in design, functionality, or colors MUST be approved by the user.
4. **Safety:** All data transitions must be logged in `.tmp/`.
5. **Reliability:** Deterministic logic only; no guessing on business rules.

## Architectural Invariants
1. **3-Layer Architecture (A.N.T.):**
   - Layer 1: SOPs in `architecture/`.
   - Layer 2: Decision making in Antigravity.
   - Layer 3: Atomic Python tools in `tools/`.
2. **State Tracking:** Every step must update `progress.md`.
